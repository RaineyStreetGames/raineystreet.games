About Me

I'm Jesse Michael. I'm a software engineer in Arizona who builds backend systems and cloud services for a living.

I also make games.

Most of what I build is small right now. Small projects, small ideas, things I can explore and learn from. With a desire to do much more.

Since I was a kid, I've always wanted to develop games. With a head full of ideas of games that I would want to make. And now I have 3 kids and want to build games that they could play too.

And Rainey Street Games is me finally doing it.

About the Studio

Rainey Street Games will focus on slow, laid-back, and exploratory gameplay.

Small experiences. Worlds you can wander through and spend time in. Games built around curiosity, discovery, and the feeling of being somewhere for a while.

We won't deliver games built around twitchy reflexes, fast-paced action, or hyper-competitive gameplay.

The goal is to build places you can transport yourself into. Places that feel imaginative, cozy, and worth exploring at your own pace.
